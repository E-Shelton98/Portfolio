Hello, I'm Erik!

I'm an avid tech-head who loves to continually learn and solve complex problems. I started out teaching myself code in 2017 and decided to attend General Assembly in June of 2020. I started class in august of 2020 as part of a fully remote course. Since then I have been working on building multiple personal projects to grow my portfolio, as well as beginning to look into contributing to open source. I see myself as confident in my abilities while understanding there is much to still learn, very curious about the world around me, and consistently striving to help share my knowledge with those who ask to help them grow.
